Detailed Description

Matei Burlacu is a tourist guide, fresh graduate of the UAIC Faculty of Geography and Geology.

He is young and new in this domain. He has little experience in the field, working for a tourism agency.

He has tried using different application and tools to draw clients to his tours, including promoting himself on social media.

He feels the need for an application which helps the tourist to be immersed in his tours. He also wants to freely communicate with his clients, to be able to bend to their needs.

His practical goals are to make it easier for people to find about him and to present his services as attractive as possible.

His personal goals are to not bore people on his tour and to give his occupation a fresh and personal take.

His business goals are to increase the number of tourists that choose his tours and their satisfaction with what they learn.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Matei wants to develop a new tour. He opens the application, logins and searches for POI in Iasi. He checks attraction by attraction, looking at reviews and number of visits, until he comes up with a theme for his tour. He then chooses which attractions should be part of the tour, establishes a route and the timing for the tour, among other details. Now with the tour in place, he begins customizing. He sets a name for the tour, a thumbnail. He records audio guides for each POI. Now with everything done, he posts the tour.

Scenario 2:

Matei wants to update today's series of a tour. He open the application. He opens his schedule and selects the tour he wants to edit. He selects the POI he wants to remove, because it is closed today. He selects it, and removes it. A message is send on the tour series' group. He also sends an additional message clarifying the situation.
